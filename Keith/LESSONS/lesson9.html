Media literacy and information literacy have an impact, on aspects of our lives such as personal, political, economic, educational, social and professional domains. On a level they enhance our quality of life by facilitating communication, easy access to information and convenient research. They also play a role in strengthening family connections through video calls and providing entertainment choices. In the arena media literacy promotes engagement and collaborative decision making. From a perspective it creates opportunities for content creation and monetization that contribute to a thriving economy. In the field of education it transforms learning environments by tailoring resources to learning styles. Socially speaking individuals with media literacy skills form groups that foster interaction, through networking sites while supporting advocacies. These literacies are dynamic and multifaceted as they shape and enrich aspects of our lives.
